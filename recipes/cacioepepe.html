Cacio e Pepe

Description

An easy to make and delicious cacio e pepe recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dry fusilli pasta
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta until "al dente".
  2. Drain the pasta keeping 1/2 cup of cooking water and set aside.
  3. In a large saut√© pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add in the pepper and cook until fragrant and toasted, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the pasta water and bring to a boil. Add in the pasta and toss to coat.
  5. Turn the heat down to low and add in the cheeses. Toss the pasta continually until the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve topped generously with more cheese and pepper, if desired.

Brownies

Description

Quick and Easy Brownies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flourr
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup walnut crushed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Mix all Ingredients until combined.
  3. Bake in oven for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Let it cool down before slicing and serving
